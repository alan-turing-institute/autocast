# Community

The AutoCast community is a group of users and contributors who are interested in spatiotemporal forecasting and the development of AutoCast. The community is open to anyone who is interested.

We are very much welcoming contributions to the package. To get started, please read the [Contributing Guide](./contributing.md).

Feel free to reach out to us at [ai4physics@turing.ac.uk](mailto:ai4physics@turing.ac.uk) for queries about AutoCast or ideas for collaboration.
