# Ethical guidance

AutoCast is a research framework for spatiotemporal modelling of physical
systems. The examples, datasets, pretrained models, and tutorials in this
repository are scoped to socially beneficial domains such as weather and
climate forecasting, and physical simulation.

The MIT License places no restrictions on use. We nonetheless discourage
harmful or unethical applications of AutoCast.

Users extending the framework are encouraged to document the data and
training regime of any derived model and to communicate model uncertainty
and limitations to downstream decision-makers.
